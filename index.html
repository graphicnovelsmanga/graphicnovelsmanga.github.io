NARUTO

Naruto picture Naruto is an amazing story about ninjas and is one of the best books i have ever read. It is written by an amazing author called Masashi Kishimoto and it has a excellent plot and it is a great anime as well. All the characters are really well developed and i could never stop reading it.There are 72 volumes so it took quite a long time to read but it was definitely worth the read.It was a big craze between me and my friends at school when someone brought in the first 20 volumes and then we took it in turns buying the next books.The ending is great and very satisfying so if you have the chance i sincerely recommend it.
